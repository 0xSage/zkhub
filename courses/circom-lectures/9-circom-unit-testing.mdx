## Description

A hands-on workshop where we upgrade a fork of TornadoCash to use the Poseidon hash. We also demonstrate how to unit-test your circuits and contracts.

## Pre-Session Reading/Setup

We recommend you inspect the TornadoCash core codebase here: [https://github.com/tornadocash/tornado-core/](https://github.com/tornadocash/tornado-core/)

CC has also included session notes here: [https://hackmd.io/ISJ8sw7HTgysMNe0hW8iTA](https://hackmd.io/ISJ8sw7HTgysMNe0hW8iTA)
